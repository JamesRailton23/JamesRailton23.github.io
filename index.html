About Me

My name is James Railton, I am an enthusiastic and passionate student currently seeking both higher (level 4) and degree (level 6) apprenticeships within IT-related positions. Within IT, my areas of speciality include Cloud Computing and Software engineering, however, I do have a good level of experience in other areas such as Computer Networking, Linux Systems and Cybersecurity.

I am currently working as a Volunteer Administrative Assistant at the NHS. This role has enabled me to gain valuable workplace skills while undertaking assigned projects. Furthermore, as a volunteer, I participate in volunteering events these include meeting with key figures within the local community to advocate for volunteering within the NHS and also meeting with the general public and answering any questions they may have about volunteering within the NHS.

To deepen my knowledge of the workplace and IT, I actively engage in self-led learning to enhance my knowledge and skills. This includes undertaking personal development courses on topics I wish to learn more about and courses that relate to employment and professional workplace skills. These courses have not only allowed me to develop as an IT professional but an individual and have equipped me with the necessary skills to succeed within the workplace.

During my spare time, I enjoy a variety of different hobbies and activities. My main hobby that I participate in is flight simulation on my computer. This hobby has combined both my love and passion for aviation and computing into a single hobby. Flight simulation has allowed me to indulge in my love of flying highly technical pieces of modern engineering in a virtualised representation of our entire world. Other hobbies and activities that I enjoy undertaking include golf, cycling and travelling the world to experience new cultures.

My aspiration for the future is to become a successful apprentice within an IT company, that works on projects within a team and can utilise the skills that I have acquired through my education and professional development courses to deliver high-quality products for clients.
